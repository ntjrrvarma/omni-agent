Standard Operating Procedure for Freezer Management

1. Temperature Monitoring:
   - Freezers must maintain temperatures between -80°C and -60°C.
   - Check temperatures daily.

2. Failure Response:
   - If temperature drops below -70°C, no immediate action required.
   - If below -80°C, alert maintenance team.
   - Document all failures in the log.

3. Maintenance:
   - Annual calibration required.
   - Clean coils monthly.

This document complies with GxP guidelines.
